Every major financial app was designed for the same person: an English-speaking, middle-income American who already has a bank account and parents who talked about money at dinner.

Avanza was built for them. And for everyone who deserves financial education that actually speaks to their real life.

What is a credit score?

Tu score de crédito, entendido.

Your credit score is a number between 300 and 850. It tells banks how reliably you repay borrowed money. Higher score = more doors open: better apartments, lower interest rates, more financial freedom.

⭐ Cultural note: Many Latino immigrants arrive with strong credit history from their home country, but it doesn't transfer to the US. You start at zero. That is completely normal. Only 39% of Hispanic Americans are financially literate (P-Fin Index 2025). This lesson is the first step to changing that.

STEP 2 OF 4+30 XP
📊 5 Factors

How your score is calculated

Conoce estos 5 y controla tu futuro.

Your score isn't random. It comes from 5 specific factors, know them and you can improve on purpose.

35%
Payment History

Do you pay on time? The #1 factor.

30%
Credit Utilization

How much of your limit are you using? Keep it under 30%.

15%
Length of History

Longer credit history = better score.

10%
Credit Mix

Cards and loans, diversity helps.

10%
New Credit

Applying for too much at once temporarily hurts.
